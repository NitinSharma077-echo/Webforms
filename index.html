About Petrotek

Engineering supplies and lubrication solutions, built for the Gulf's working machines.

Petrotek General Trading LLC has supplied industrial lubricants, greases and engineering equipment across the UAE since 2007. What started as a lubricants distributor has grown into a full-line supplier of oils, greases, compressors, lubrication equipment, chains and sprockets — stocked and shipped door-to-door across the GCC.

Our showroom in Mussafah M17, Abu Dhabi, stocks everything from food-grade lubricants and vacuum pumps to flow meters and precision measuring tools — backed by a technical team that helps operations teams match the right product to the right machine, not just the cheapest one on the shelf.
